New Kasama has so much to offer.

Birding in Lusaka by Eugene Booysen

First of all, who knew that just outside Lusaka you could find such rich miombo woodland. I mean, I have been to Lusaka many times but have not had the time in those visits to really explore the surroundings. I have not explored the south, west or north but heading east into the leopards hill area, is incredible.

To access this area you will have to know where to find Kwisoko Game Ranch. Drive down the gravel road until you find a stable. From there onwards you can explore the various areas of this place. There are some animals to be found, like smaller antelopes and baboons.

In the early hours of the morning it is easily understood why you would believe that this place is a birding paradise. The morning chorus is so loud and you can hear the numeros bird calls in this area. I ahve always wanted to see the African Spotted Creeper, and here I managed to see a breeding pair with a youngster. This place is incredible.

I would definitly recommend this place to anyone wanting to have a birding day out from the capital. There is another cool place just around the corner called lazy j which is also incredible for birding. But you can not compare the two. At least they are close to one another and you can make two trips of it he he.
